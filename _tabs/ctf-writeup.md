# CTF Writeup

Welcome to the CTF Writeup category! Here you'll find detailed writeups of Capture The Flag challenges I've solved.

## What are CTF Writeups?

CTF writeups are detailed explanations of how I approached and solved various cybersecurity challenges. They include:

- **Challenge Analysis**: Understanding the problem
- **Tools Used**: What tools and techniques were employed
- **Step-by-Step Solutions**: Detailed walkthroughs
- **Lessons Learned**: Key takeaways and tips
